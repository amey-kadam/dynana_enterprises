About Dnyana Enterprises

Our Company

Dnyana Enterprises stands as a leading contractor in Civil, Fabrication, and Erection services, specializing in comprehensive industrial solutions. Our expertise spans structural work, piping, tanks, vessels, and equipment installation across major industrial sectors.

Currently executing projects at JSW Steel Limited in Dolvi, we have successfully completed complex assignments including:

  • Fabrication and Erection of Ducts and Structures
  • High-Pressure Pipeline Installations
  • Maintenance Job Works
  • Stacker/Crane Rail Alignment
  • Turnkey Civil and Interior Projects
